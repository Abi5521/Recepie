Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This Apple Pie recepie has been in my family for generations and never fail to delight.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced
    • Instructions
  • Preheat oven to 375[deg]F (190[deg]C).
  • In a large bowl, mix together sugar and cinnamon.
  • Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
